Edible items for qb-core and QBox. Makes it so you have more items in your server. Including food/drinks/alcahol, hunting, hacking, usbs, ids, memberships, mechanic, illegal, materials, drugs, etc

REQUIREMENTS:

latest qb-core build qb-smallresources or latest QBox build

qb-core INSTALLATION:

STEP 1 Put images into inventory image folder (any qb inventory will work)

STEP 2 copy the items from qb-core/items.lua paste the items into your qb-core/shared/items.lua (at the bottom)

STEP 3 copy the contents from qb-smallresources/config paste the contents into your qb-smallrecources/config

QBox Installation

STEP 1: Put images into your inventory image folder, ox_inventory/web/images

STEP 2: Copy the items from QBox_items.lua and paste them at the bottom in your ox_inventory/data/items

STEP 3: copy the contents from the qbox/smallresources/config and paste the conents into your qbx/qbx_smallresources/qbx_consumables/config
